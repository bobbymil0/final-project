About Grovi

Your digital companion for successful plant parenting

Why Grovi?

Grovi was created out of a simple need: helping plant lovers keep their green friends alive and thriving. Too many beautiful plants have met their demise due to forgotten watering schedules or lack of proper care tracking. Our mission is to make plant care simple, organized, and enjoyable for everyone.
