Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my plant data? +

Your plant data is stored locally in your browser. To reset, you can clear your browser's local storage or use the reset option in the Plant Tracker page.

Can I export my plant data? +

Currently, data is stored locally. We're working on export/import features for future updates.

What if my plant isn't in the type list? +

You can select "Other" as the plant type and add specific details in the care notes section.
